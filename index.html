Find Your Way to Savory Haven

Nestled in the heart of downtown, Savory Haven is your neighborhood retreat for freshly-baked goodness and cozy vibes. You'll find us on Maple Avenue, just a short stroll from Central Park—right where the city's energy meets a welcoming calm. Whether you're a local or just passing through, our doors are always open to offer you a warm smile and a comforting cup of coffee. Drop by, and let the aroma of our ovens guide you home.

Since 2021, Savory Haven has been crafting delicious, artisan baked goods and expertly-prepared brews using only the finest ingredients. From our oven to your table—freshness and flavor in every bite!
