OUR STORY

In September 2020, during the height of the pandemic, we watched as frontliners worked tirelessly to keep our community safe. Wanting to show our gratitude, we brewed up a plan—literally. One of us, a former barista at the beloved MoonMoon's Café, dusted off an old espresso machine, and together, we started handing out free coffee and pastries to healthcare workers and essential personnel in our neighborhood.

What began as small acts of kindness quickly grew. By February 2021, our homemade brews and baked goods had gained a loyal following, and we found ourselves running a cozy at-home business. Orders poured in from friends, family, and neighbors who had heard about our story and wanted a taste of the coffee and pastries we had shared so freely.

In August 2021, we took a leap of faith and opened our first brick-and-mortar store. What started as a tiny space with a couple of tables has since expanded into a warm and welcoming café where everyone—from frontliners to first-time visitors—feels right at home.

Our mission remains the same: to serve great coffee, delicious pastries, and kindness in every cup.

OUR VISION.

At Savory Haven, we craft exceptional coffee and baked goods with care, using quality ingredients and time-honored techniques. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where neighbors become friends, and every guest feels at home.

To be the heart of our community, where every cup, bite, and conversation inspires connection, comfort, and a sense of belonging.
